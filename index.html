Some Tapped

It was late night and I was walking alone on a road and suddenly someone tapped my shoulder, I turned back but there was no one. Next day again it was late night, I was walking on the same road and once again someone tapped me, I turned back and this time also no one was there, so I was very inquisitive to find out the person who was actually doing this prank on me. I started searching him in nearby places, and suddenly I saw a watchman walking in front of a big and old bunglow with a lantern in his hand. I went near him and inquired who stayed in that bunglow? He replied that the bunglow belonged to an engineer named Mr. Chetan Das and then I shared him about the incident which was happening with me since the last two days and he literally laughed at me and said that "My boy please dont roam around here at this hour of time".And suddenly someone called him from inside so he went and I too left the place. I thought of coming back to the bunglow the very next day and have a conversation with the owner about the incident which happened with me. So the next morning I went and I was literally shocked to see hat the place looked very different then it was yesterday night. The bunglow looked much older than yesterday, it was dirty, dusty, full of spider webs. Nearby I saw a tea stall, I went there and approached people there and asked them about the bunglow and they told me that the bunglow has been locked since the last ten years. But I couldn't believe them. I went to the bunglow again and I was shocked to see the name plate where it was written "Late Chetan Das"....

The end of your serach is here.
